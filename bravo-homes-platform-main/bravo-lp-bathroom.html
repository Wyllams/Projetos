FAQ

Common Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before getting started with your bathroom remodel.

Bathroom remodel costs in Georgia vary based on scope and materials. A basic refresh (vanity, toilet, paint) runs $3,000–$8,000. A mid-range remodel with new tile, shower, and fixtures typically costs $10,000–$20,000. A full luxury renovation with custom tile, soaking tub, and high-end finishes can run $25,000–$50,000+. We provide free in-home estimates so you get an accurate price for your specific project — no guessing.
Timeline depends on project size. A simple refresh takes 3–5 days. A standard remodel (new shower, tile, vanity) usually takes 1–2 weeks. A full gut-and-rebuild takes 2–4 weeks. We give you a firm timeline before starting and stick to it. Most projects are completed on or ahead of schedule. During your estimate, we'll give you an exact timeline for your project.
It depends on what's being done. Cosmetic updates (tile, vanity, fixtures) typically don't require permits. However, moving plumbing, electrical, or structural work does require permits in Cobb County and surrounding areas. As a licensed Georgia contractor, we pull all necessary permits on your behalf — it's included in our scope of work. You'll never have to worry about compliance or inspections; we handle everything.
Our free in-home estimate includes a full walkthrough of your bathroom, an honest assessment of what needs to be done, material options for every budget, a detailed written quote with line-item pricing, and a realistic project timeline. There is zero obligation to sign. We show up on time, listen carefully, and give you all the information you need to make an informed decision — whether you hire us or not.
Yes! We partner with trusted financing providers to offer flexible payment options, including 0% interest promotional periods for qualified applicants. You can get the bathroom of your dreams without paying everything upfront. Ask about financing during your free estimate and we'll walk you through all available options — in English or Spanish, whichever you prefer. Monthly payments often start as low as $150/month.
